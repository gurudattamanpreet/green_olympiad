About Us

Green Zone Olympiad was established in 2018, and since then we have been committed to providing quality education. Our mission is to develop students with academic excellence, moral values, and social responsibility.

We have a team of experienced teachers who focus on the individual development of students. Our curriculum includes modern teaching methods and technology to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Olympiad not only focuses on academic excellence but also encourages sports, arts, and cultural activities so that students can develop holistically.

🌿 Awards & Recognitions

🧒 For Students

Certificate of Participation

Every participating student will receive an Official Certificate of Participation from the Green Zone Olympiad.

School Topper Award

The School Topper will be awarded a Medal, Certificate, and an Achievement Shield in recognition of outstanding performance.

District Rank Holders

Top 10 District Rank Holders will be honored by Government Dignitaries during the Annual Principal Meet, a prestigious event held once every year.

State Rank Holders

State Rank Holders will be rewarded with an exclusive National Educational Tour.

National Rank Holders

National Rank Holders will get the lifetime opportunity of an International Educational Trip representing India globally.

🎓 For Principals

Individual Award of Excellence

Every Principal will receive an Individual Award of Excellence at the Annual Principal Meet hosted at Hotel Lemon Tree.

Certification of Appreciation

Principals will also be honored with a Certification of Appreciation for their continuous efforts in introducing innovative activities and fostering the holistic development of students.

🏫 For Schools

Official Recognition

Participating Schools will be presented with an Official Certificate of Participation and a Prestigious School Shield to be displayed in their Award Gallery.

This recognition celebrates the school's commitment towards promoting student development through the Green Zone Olympiad.
